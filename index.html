Logitech G633 vs G933

tldr; G933 is very bad.

I bought the wired [Logitech G633 ($199)][1] and wireless [Logitech G933 ($249)][2] headsets, with the intent to try both and shameless return one of them.

The results surprised me; the wireless G933 had a steady static sound in the background, which got very audible as the volume increased, but which was still very obvious even at normal listening levels.

I can't easily caputre the difference in how they produce sound, but I can easily capture how *awful* the microphone is on the more expensive wireless G933.
