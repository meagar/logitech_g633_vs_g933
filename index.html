Logitech G633 vs G933

tldr; G933 is very bad.

I bought the wired Logitech G633 ($199) and wireless Logitech G933 ($249) headsets, with the intent to try both and shamelessly return one of them.

The results surprised me; the wireless G933 had a steady static sound in the background, which got very audible as the volume increased, but which was still very obvious even at normal listening levels.

I can't easily caputre the difference in how they produce sound, but I can easily capture how *awful* the microphone is on the more expensive wireless G933.

The wireless G933's mic

The wired G633's mic

If you can't hear a difference, turn up your volume. The G933 is extremely noisy.

Both recordings were made on a 2015 Macbook Pro, seconds apart, using the same hardware and software. The same noise persists on my Windows 10 desktop.
